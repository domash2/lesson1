Around the world

"A powerful eruption of Colima Volcano in Mexico on December 13, 2015. That night, the weather was dry and cold, friction of ash particles generated lightning of about 600 meters that connected ash and volcano, and illuminated most of the dark scene. In 2015, this volcano showed a lot of eruptive activity with ash explosions that raised 2-3 km above the crater. Most of the night explosions produced incandescent rockfalls and lightning no bigger than 100 meters on average." – Sergio Tapiro Velasco
